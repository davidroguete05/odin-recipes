Hawaiian Pizza

Description

Hawaiian pizza is an interpretation of the traditional jam and cheese pizza, adding chopped pineapple pieces making the pizza a bit sweeter than others. It's well received in the colombian caribbean coast and my favorite BTW.

Ingredients

  • 500 g all-purpose flour
  • 10 g active dry yeast
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 300 ml warm water
  • 200 g pizza tomato sauce
  • 300 g shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 200 g sliced or diced ham
  • 1 cup pineapple chunks (fresh or canned, well drained)
  • Dried oregano to taste

Steps

  1. In a small bowl, combine the warm water, active dry yeast, and sugar. Stir gently and let it sit for 8–10 minutes until a foamy layer forms on top, indicating that the yeast is activated.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour and salt. Make a well in the center and pour in the activated yeast mixture along with the olive oil.
  3. Mix with a spoon until the dough starts coming together. Then transfer to a lightly floured surface and knead for 8–10 minutes until the dough becomes smooth, elastic, and slightly tacky but not sticky.
  4. Place the dough in a lightly oiled bowl, cover with a clean kitchen towel or plastic wrap, and let it rise in a warm place for 60–90 minutes, or until it doubles in size.
  5. Preheat the oven to 220°C (430°F). If using a pizza stone, place it inside the oven while preheating.
  6. Once the dough has risen, punch it down gently to release air. Roll it out on a floured surface into a round shape about 30–35 cm (12–14 inches) in diameter. Transfer it to a baking tray or parchment paper.
  7. Spread the pizza tomato sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges for the crust.
  8. Sprinkle half of the mozzarella cheese over the sauce. Distribute the ham evenly across the surface, followed by the pineapple chunks. Add the remaining mozzarella on top.
  9. Sprinkle dried oregano lightly over the pizza for added aroma and flavor.
  10. Bake for 12–15 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is fully melted and slightly bubbly. Remove from the oven, let it rest for a few minutes, slice, and serve hot.
